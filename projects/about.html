About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to produce organically grown, healthy and affordable poultry products and other exceptional agricultural services to all our customers

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading provider of poultry products, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability, while fostering a strong connection with our community and environment. ElevenKay Farms Nigeria Ltd envisions a country that will solely depend on our processed farm products and supply the much needed states with our farm produce.

Our History

Founded in 2000, our poultry farm has grown from a small family operation to a reputable provider of high-quality poultry products. With years of experience and dedication, we have maintained our core values of integrity, excellence, and sustainability.

Frequently Asked Questions

On our poultry farm, we raise a variety of birds, including chickens, turkeys, and ducks. Our chickens consist of both egg-laying hens and broiler chickens raised for meat. We also have a flock of heritage breed turkeys and a small number of Pekin and Muscovy ducks. We take pride in the diversity of poultry on our farm and the high-quality products we are able to provide to our customers.

At our poultry farm, the welfare and humane treatment of our animals is of the utmost importance. We follow strict guidelines and industry best practices to ensure our birds are raised in a stress-free, comfortable environment. This includes providing ample space, clean living conditions, access to fresh water and nutritious feed, and opportunities for natural behaviors such as foraging and dust-bathing. We also work closely with veterinarians to monitor the health and well-being of our flock. Our commitment to ethical and responsible animal husbandry is a core part of our values as a sustainable, family-owned poultry farm.
