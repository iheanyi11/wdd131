Welcome to ElevenKay Farms Limited

ElevenKay Farms Nigeria Ltd is a privately owned indigenous Farm company located at the heart of Oshodi-isolo Local Government Area in Lagos State Nigeria. The company was established with the sole aim of generating economic activities, capacity building in agriculture, job creation, poverty eradication and revenue generation. We commenced farming business over a decade now, however, the company was incorporated by the Corporate Affairs Commission in May 2016.

BATTERY CAGE SYSTEM

ElevenKay Farms focus on efficiency in the production of poultry products. We use the Battery Cage Systems for our egg-laying birds. This system which is perceived to be the best approach in housing of egg-laying birds.
